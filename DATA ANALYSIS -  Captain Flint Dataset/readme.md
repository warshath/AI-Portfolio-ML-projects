EMC Data Consultants is a reputed business consultancy group that uses business data to provide consultancy on business process improvement. Captain Flint Distillery is a well reputed distillery that produces whiskey, rum, beer, wine and other alcoholic beverages. The company has faced stiff competition over the years from different other breweries and as such is aiming at improving their products and sales. Hence, they have hired EMC Data Consultants to collect data on customers and identify the ideal customer profile for the company to advertise and cater so as to improve profits by reducing the expenditures. Accordingly, EMC has collected data on customers and their buying behaviours and intends to use this data to provide an ideal customer segmentation. The data collected was mainly carried out using datasets available from a large scale shopping complex. A second set of data was collected from Captain Flint about their performance and sales throughout the globe about their wine sales.
